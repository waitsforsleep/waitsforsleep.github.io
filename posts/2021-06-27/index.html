Today I Learned about the “Lindy effect” which postulates that the future life expectancy of non-perishable things like memes(in the original Richard Dawkins definition of the word) or practices is proportional to their current age.

Also worth perusing a newsletter about this by a dude who tries to re-interpret a lot of modern habits (especially those surrounding diet, sleep, hygiene) through the lens of the Lindy Effect.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lindy_effect
