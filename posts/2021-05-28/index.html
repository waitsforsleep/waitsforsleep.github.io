Write up that chronicles India’s slow motion train-wreck with its indigenously developed Covid vaccine which uses the same inactivated virus tech as the Chinese vaccines.

Another related but interesting point I keep encountering when looking into Covid vaccines is that, logically the inactivated vaccines are the ones one would expect to hold up well against variants, as opposed to mRNA/Adenovirus vector based vaccines that are more preoccupied with Covid-19’s spike protein than the virus itself. But real world efficacy data from clinical trials seem to indicate the opposite.
