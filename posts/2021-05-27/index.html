Interesting interview and idea regarding mindsets (scout vs soldier). Basic premise seems to be that the soldier mindset is obsessed with changing or attacking or defending beliefs and situations (assumed to be for the better), where as the scout mindset is centered around consciously trying to build a better “map” or with seeing things as they are instead of attacking or defending. I think I will buy her book “The Scout Mindset: Why Some People See Things Clearly and Others Don’t” and give it a read, premise sounds interesting. Even though I grew up idolizing the solider, I’m at a place where I think the explorer might be the cooler one.

https://noahpinion.substack.com/p/interview-julia-galef
